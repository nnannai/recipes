Ingredients:
 - even number slices of bread
 - tin of sardines
 - butter
 - pepper to taste
 - a pinch of salt
 - an egg per two slices of bread

Directions:
 1. butter both sides of the bread slices (as much as u want)
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the sardines, eggs, salt and pepper. Mix until smooth. 
    spread batter onto each slice of bread.
 
 3. sandwich toast for minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean.
